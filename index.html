Story

In the ever-evolving landscape of the 21st-century job market, the recruitment process has become a challenging task for many businesses. Traditional hiring methods are time-consuming, expensive, and often biased, leading to less than optimal outcomes for both employers and job seekers.

Enter our innovative startup. Inspired by the power of AI and the desire to streamline the hiring process, we embarked on a journey to transform the recruitment industry. With the primary objectives of speed, fairness, and cost-efficiency, we developed an AI-powered platform designed to overcome the traditional obstacles of hiring.

How it's works

With SmartPost, RapidInterview, and QuickReview, our platform makes hiring a breeze – simply submit a job description, let AI handle the interviews, and swiftly review insightful evaluations, all with just a few clicks

1. SmartPost

You submit the comprehensive job description on our platform. This includes the responsibilities, qualifications, and key skills required for the job.

Our advanced AI system thoroughly understands the job description to prepare for the subsequent step.

2. RapidInterview

Once the job description is submitted, our AI takes the lead. It interacts with each candidate, asking 10-20 relevant questions tailored from the job description and their resume.

This intelligent interview process ensures each candidate is evaluated based on the essential qualifications and skills required for the job.

3. QuickReview

Following the AI-led interviews, the platform provides an easy-to-understand review of each candidate. It presents scores, notable strengths, and areas of improvement for each candidate.

This concise and systematic review allowing you to make informed decisions quickly and confidently.
